About Me

My name is Tristan Benedict-Hall, I live in Richmond VA with my wife and our daughter. I currently work at the Apple Store behind the Genius Bar and am currently attending The University of Richmond’s Coding Bootcamp.

My interest in web development started while studying graphic design at The Maryland Institute College of Art. Primarily my focus of studies revolved around printmaking but I always enjoyed creating websites for myself and friends. I spent a number of years working in the graphic design industry but am looking to take those skills further which is why I am so excited to be a part of the University of Richmond’s inagurial coding bootcamp program.
